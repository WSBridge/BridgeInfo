Letters to the Willow Springs HOA Board in support of the proposed bridge

Supporters distributed flyers to all of the units in Willow Springs, urging unit owners to write to our HOA Board, in response to an invitation from the HOA Board.

The invitation by the Board was sent by email on January 30, and stated:

  • If you are unable to attend [a meeting on February 21 or March 2] or would rather not speak, send your feedback/questions prior to the meeting via email to WillowSprings@westwindmanagement.com where it will get captured and posted with both meetings' feedback.

Unfortunately, Bruce Lobmeyer, the HOA President, now says he will not post the text of the letters the Board has received. However, many of the people who responded to our flyer copied us at WillowSpringsPedestrianBridge@proton.me. We will post the text of these letters below, as soon as we get permission of the authors of the letters.
